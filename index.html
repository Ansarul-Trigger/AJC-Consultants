Who We Are

Decades of Expertise. One Trusted Partner.

AJC Consultants was built on a foundation of hard work, family, and an unshakable belief that the right opportunity—seized at the right moment—can change everything.

Dominic R. Catalano, Principal Consultant and founder, is the son of an immigrant who came to this country with little more than determination and a dream. He built his career from the ground up, learning every layer of the construction industry before creating AJC Consultants.

Alongside him is his wife and co-owner, Lisa Catalano—whose resilience, work ethic, and sharp business instincts have helped shape the company into what it is today.

Together, they built more than a business—they built a legacy. And they bring that same commitment, integrity, and personal investment to every project they take on.

What We Do

Full-Spectrum Construction Consulting

From the first site survey to the final inspection, AJC Consultants ensures every piece is in place and every professional is the right fit.

Project Planning & Oversight

We manage end-to-end project planning—timelines, milestones, permitting, and budget oversight— so your build stays on track from day one.

Contractor & Builder Sourcing

Access our network of vetted, licensed contractors and builders matched to your project type, scale, and budget. No guesswork, no surprises.

Legal & Compliance Coordination

We connect you with experienced construction attorneys who handle contracts, zoning disputes, liability, and regulatory compliance.

Commercial & Industrial Builds

Skyscrapers, warehouses, storage facilities, office complexes—our expertise spans the full spectrum of commercial and industrial construction.

Site Evaluation & Feasibility

Before breaking ground, we assess site conditions, local regulations, environmental factors, and structural feasibility to minimize risk.

Professional Team Assembly

One call connects you to a complete team—architects, engineers, subcontractors, and specialists— curated specifically for your project.
